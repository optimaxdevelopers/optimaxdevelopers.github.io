New R K Garden

About New R K Garden

It is a Vasthu perfect model project consisting sites of various dimensions. It has an excellent water source with 2 Bore wells providing copious Water. Pipe Lines of standard quality are laid to provide water to each plot and the 55 cents Park area. The entire land has been fenced with barbed wire and chain link with a gate. The layout is in a very pleasant, picturesque and calm atmosphere. Thus one can construct a house or retirement home at once.


The Tar Roads are constructed with good quality material. Street lights are provided in adequate numbers. The roads and park areas have been handed over to Local body who gives plan approval for house construction. Thus one can get Bank Loan for the purchase of plots and construction of houses. Saplings are being planted with a view to go Green. This Gated Community project has a full time watchman/Gardener and is located very close to coconut farms which keeps the weather cool all round the year.
